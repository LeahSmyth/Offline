OFFLINE is the leading app for digital detoxing, designed to help users reduce the amount of time they spend on their mobile devices.

Offering a range of detox options, OFFLINE will allow users to pick a detox that’s relevant to them. It also allows users to track how much time they spend on their phones, highlighting the need for a detox!

Rewards are important to us all, that’s why OFFLINE award badges for each detox completed. What’s more, users can track their phone usage to understand why detoxes are important.

No excuses, become one of the millions of people who are having a break from their phones and start enjoying the real moments of life!

Alternatives

The Alternatives detox encourages you to select an alternative activity as your detox, such as going for a walk or reading a book, in order to completely step away from your phone and to spend time doing something productive or developing a new hobby.

Focus

The Focus detox allows you to set a timer for long you wish to stay focused for. While the timer is activated you cannot access your phone or any of its apps, allowing more focused time, particularly at work, university or school.

Bedtime

The Bedtime detox allows you to set a bedtime and a waking time. During these hours you cannot use your phone – this is aimed at helping to improve sleeping patterns. The app will give you a 15 minute warning before your bedtime.

Boundaries

The Boundaries detox allows you to draw a plan of your house, you then select rooms that you aren’t allowed to use your phone in, if you use your phone in these rooms, your phone will vibrate until you lock it again. This is aimed at improving relationships with your family or housemates.

Digital Holiday

The Digital Holiday detox allows you to choose how many days you would like to refrain from using your phone for each month. You can then select dates to suit you. Total detox from your phone will be helpful for the body and mind.

Breathe

The Breathe detox will ask you to take deep breathes for a time selection of your choice, before you do anything. This will help you to focus on your breathing and assist in stressful situations.
